Pinkie Swear Ice Cream

Branding, illustration, and packaging design for a line of vegan oat milk ice cream. The name makes good on a childhood pinkie swear between sisters Amy & Mei to make delicious ice cream together one day.

  • Who am I?

    Heya! My name is Kat! I absolutely adore all things design, and if not stopped, I could sit at the computer designing for hours on end without realizing where all the time has gone. As much a sucker for good packaging design as roguelite video games, I always inject a little personality into everything I do.
